Kamala Harris

The first female, African American and Indian American Vice President of the United States in history.

Here's a timeline of Kamala's career so far:

  • 1964 - Kamala Devi Harris was born October 20, in Oakland, CA, to parents Shyamala Gopalan and Donald J. Harris.
  • 1986 - Graduated from Howard University with a degree in political science and economics.
  • 1989 - Graduated from University of California, Hastings College of the Law
  • 1990 - Was admitted to the California Bar in June.
  • 2004 - Became district attorney of San Francisco.
  • 2011 - Appointed attorney general of California
  • 2017 - U.S. senator from California
  • 2021 - Vice president of the United States
